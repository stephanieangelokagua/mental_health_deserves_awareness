Mental Health Deserves Awareness

Take the first step in taking care of your mind today!

Mental health is a positive concept related to the social and emotional wellbeing of people and communities. The concept relates to the enjoyment of life, ability to cope with stress and sadness, the fulfilment of goals and potential, and a sense of connection to others. Mental health is about wellness rather than illness and is not merely the absence of a mental health condition.

Stay empowered on your mental wellness journey with regular insights, tips, and resources delivered right to your inbox. Join our community today and take the first step towards a healthier, balanced mind!

Signs

Mood swings or constantly feeling low

Feeling irritable, over-sensitive or aggressive

Lack of care for personal appearance or personal responsibilities

Tips

Learn to manage stress

Finding ways to manage stress is essential for a peaceful mind and body. Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing, yoga, or meditation. Explore activities that make you feel calm, from art to nature walks.

Get enough sleep and rest

Sleep plays a crucial role in both our physical and mental well-being. Prioritizing sleep can improve focus, energy, and resilience to stress. Remember, quality rest is a key foundation for a balanced, healthy life.

Support

NHS Talking Therapies

NHS Talking Therapies provides free, confidential mental health support for people dealing with anxiety, depression, and other common mental health concerns. You can self-refer to NHS Talking Therapies without needing a GP referral. Simply visit the NHS website to find your local IAPT service, or speak with your GP for guidance on the best options available.

NHS Talking Therapies

Mind UK

Mind is a leading mental health charity in the UK that provides information, advice, and support for anyone experiencing a mental health problem. Visit the Mind website to explore their resources, or call their infoline at 0300 123 3393 for advice and information. Mind also has local branches across the UK, offering in-person support and community services tailored to your area.

Mind UK

Samaritans

Samaritans is a charity that provides 24/7 emotional support to anyone struggling to cope, whether you're experiencing a crisis or simply need someone to talk to. You can call Samaritans anytime on 116 123, for free, from any phone in the UK or Ireland. You can also email jo@samaritans.org if you prefer to write down your thoughts, and they typically respond within 24 hours.
